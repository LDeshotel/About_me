Lauren Deshotel

Lulu here! I'm a country girl, born and raised in the sticks in central Louisiana.
I've spent my life surrounded by horses and cows and I can God honest say I'm more at home
in the pastures than I'll ever be in town. I'm independent and love having nice things and
I've never been afraid to work for what I want. I was always the barefoot wild child jumping
on the hay bales and playing with the horses from the barn loft. I'd disappear in the woods all
day with my cousins to bust beaver damns and we'd come home at night smelling like creek mud. We'd
tear our clothes on the barbed wire and stain ourselves purple in the mulberry trees. As much as
I miss childhood, I have a 7 year old son who I adore more than anyone and I'm blessed for the
opportunity to bring him up the way I was.
